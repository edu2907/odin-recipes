Pão de Queijo

  • 100ml milk
  • 40g butter
  • 200g sour tapioca flour
  • 200g grated cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt to taste

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Boil the milk and butter in a saucepan until it boils
  3. Pour the mixture on flour and mix to make a pasta
  4. Pour the grated cheese and mix. Lay the eggs and mix again. Pour the salt.
  5. Make small balls(with oiled hands) and bake on a medium heat until golden.
