Brigadeiro

A typical Brazilian candy that is popular throughout the country. It's served mainly for festive events, and can be found in bakeries and confectionaries, but also can be made at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles as needed

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, add condensed milk, butter and chocolate.
  2. Cook on a low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens and pulls away from the bottom of the pan, that takes around 10 - 15 minutes.
  3. Let brigadeiro rest until it reaches room's temperature, then make small balls with your hands and roll on chocolate sprinkles.
